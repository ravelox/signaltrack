# Screen Notes

## Report Issue

- make reporting easy

- avoid internal engineering leakage

Review questions:
- is impact selection clear?
- should workaround be buttons or a select?

## Defects List
Goal:
- fast triage
- strong scanability
- easy route into the detail page

Review questions:
- are the displayed columns the right defaults?
- do we need saved views in v1?

## Defect Detail
Goal:
- be the strongest screen in the product
- keep everything needed to move the issue forward in one place

Review questions:
- is the action rail sufficient?
- is the split between summaries clear enough?
- should linked reports live higher on the page?

## Manager Overview
Goal:

- avoid decorative charts

Review questions:
- are the summary metrics enough?
- do managers need trend spark lines or not in v1?
